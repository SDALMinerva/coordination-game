This is an experiment to study how individuals and groups make decisions. Your earnings in the experiment will depend on your own decisions, the decisions of others, and chance. If you follow the instructions carefully, you will earn a reward at the end of the experiment. During the experiment, your earnings will be tabulated by experimental points. At the end of the experiment, the points will be used to determine your final payment.

General Instructions:

The experiment consists of 24 rounds in which you and other players independently make the decision on whether to participate as a group. Examples of participation could include going to a social event (e.g., party), team sports (e.g., volleyball, football) or a political event (e.g., march, rally). These are events where you are more willing to participate if there are others participating with you. A specific type of event will not be given in this experiment. However, you will be given a similar decision to participate in a group or not, and that decision will affect the earnings you receive.

At the beginning of each round, you will be assigned to a new group that consists of 5 people total (including yourself).

Your earnings depend on your decision and the participation decisions of others in your group. In each round, each player will be assigned a threshold, T. This threshold, T, represents the number of other people that you need in order to make it worthwhile for you to participate in the group event (see earnings in a round described below).

At the end of each round, everyone in your group will be asked to make a decision to participate or not participate in the event.

Each round is independent from the other: whatever happens in a round does not affect the next round.

Earnings in a round:

Each player will be assigned a threshold either low T=1 or high T=3. Your earnings in a round depend on your threshold, your participation decision and the decisions of other players in your group. Each round is independent from the other: whatever happens in a round does not affect the next round.

1. If your threshold is T=1:

  • If you participate and at least 1 other player participates, then your earnings in the round will be {{session.config.payoff_above_threshold|c}}.
  • If you participate and no one else participates, then your earnings in the round will be {{session.config.payoff_below_threshold|c}}.
  • If you do not participate, your earnings in the round will be {{session.config.payoff_no_participate|c}} regardless of what others do.

2. If your threshold is T=3:

  • If you participate and at least 3 other players participate, then your earnings in the round will be {{session.config.payoff_above_threshold|c}}.
  • If you participate and less than 3 other players participate, then your earnings in the round will be {{session.config.payoff_below_threshold|c}}.
  • If you do not participate, your earnings in the round will be {{session.config.payoff_no_participate|c}} regardless of what others do.

Experimental Earnings:

You will be paid $7.00 for completing all the rounds. In addition, after completing all the rounds, we will choose ONE random round to add to your earnings. 1 experimental point is equivalent to 0.1 US dollars. Thus, at the end of the experiment, your total earnings would equal: $7 + $0 = $7.00, $7 + $5.00 = $12.00, or $7 + $10.00 = $17.00 depending on the outcome of the randomly chosen round. Because each round is independent from the others and equally likely to be chosen for your payment, you should pay close attention to your decisions in each round.
