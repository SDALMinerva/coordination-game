Welcome!

This is an experiment to study how individuals and groups make decisions. Your earnings in the experiment will depend on your own decisions, the decisions of others, and chance. If you follow the instructions carefully, you will earn a reward at the end of the experiment. During the experiment, your earnings will be accounted by experimental points. At the end of the experiment, the points will be used to determine your final payment.

General Instructions:

The experiment consists of you and other people independently making the decision on whether to participate in a group event such as a social event (e.g., party), team sports (e.g., volleyball, football) or a political event (e.g., march, rally). Everyone, including you, will be asked to make a decision to participate or not participate in the event. Your earnings depend on your decision and the participation decisions of others.

There are 48 rounds in which you will make this decision. Each round is independent from the other: whatever happens in a round does not affect the next round.

Threshold:

At the beginning of each round, you will be assigned to a new group that consists of 5 people total (including yourself). Each player, including you, will be assigned a threshold, T. This threshold, T, represents the number of other people that you need in order to make it worthwhile for you to participate in the group event (see earnings in a round described below). Each player will be assigned a threshold either low T=1 or high T=3. For example, if your threshold in a round is T=1, it means that you need at least 1 other person in order to make it worthwhile to participate in the group event. Likewise, if the threshold assigned to you in a round is T=3, it means that you need at least 3 other people to participate in the event.

Earnings in a round:

Your earnings in a round depend on your threshold, your participation decision and the decisions of other players:

*T=1:

If you participate and at least 1 other player participates, then your earnings in the round will be 150 ECU.

If you participate and no one else participates, then your earnings in the round will be 0 ECU.

If you do not participate, your earnings in the round will be 50 ECU regardless of what others do.

*T=3:

If you participate and at least 3 other players participate, then your earnings in the round will be 150 ECU.

If you participate and less than 3 other players participate, then your earnings in the round will be 0 ECU.

If you do not participate, your earnings in the round will be 50 ECU regardless of what others do.

Experimental Earnings: After completing all the rounds, we will choose a random round and your earnings will be based on the result of that round. 1 experimental point is equivalent to 0.03 US dollars. You will be paid $3.00 for completing all the rounds, plus a payment based on the outcome of the randomly chosen round. Thus, at the end of the experiment your total earnings, including the $3 completion fee, could equal either $3, $4.50, or $7.50.

Your Network:

Your network represents how you are connected with the rest of the people in your group. In each round, you will be connected to some or all of the other people in your group. They will be called Your friends. You will see the thresholds of your friends. You will also see a list of all the people (including you) in Your group.

All of the five people in your group will be connected to some or all of the other people in the group, and can view the thresholds of their friends. You cannot observe the connections between your friends or other people in your group.

The screen below shows an example of your network in a round; the gray line between your avatar and another avatar means that you are friends with that player. You will also see the list of your friends, and a list of all the players in your group. Note that you will be assigned to a new group in each round. The shape of your network may or may not be the same in each round.

Participation Decision:

The screen below shows the decision making area. In this part, each person in the group must decide whether to participate or not participate in the group event for this round. After making your participation decision for the round, a new round will start.

After you play all the rounds, at the end of the experiment, we will choose a random round and you will be paid based on the results of that round. Because each round has the same chance of being chosen for payment, you should pay careful attention to each round.

Summary of the Instructions:

- There are 32 rounds in the experiment.

- Each round is independent from the other: whatever happens in a round does not affect the next round.

- In each round, you will assigned to a different group of 5 people (including you).

- In each round, you will be assigned a new avatar and a new threshold.

- Your earnings in a round depend on your threshold, your participation decision and the decisions of others in your group.

- In the decision part, you must make your participation decision for the round. Once you submit your decision, a new round will start.

- You will be paid for ONE randomly chosen round. You should pay careful attention to each round.

As mentioned above, your earnings depend on your decision to participate and others’ decisions to participate. Before making the participation decision, each player will be given an opportunity to send messages to friends to reveal his/her intention to participate or not in the group event.

You have the following two messaging options to send to each one of your friends: 1)"I will participate", 2)"I will not participate". You can send a message to each friend individually or to all of your friends. Once you choose a friend and a message, click "Send Message" to send it. You will receive a confirmation each time you send a message to a friend.

Each person can send a message only to his/her own friends. No one can see other people’s messages.

Once you are done with sending messages, you can click "Continue" to proceed to the decision part. You will view the messages sent to you by your friends in the decision part. Make sure that you send all of the messages you want before clicking "Continue" as you will proceed to the decision part and cannot get back to the messaging part.
